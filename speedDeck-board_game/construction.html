Game Construction Document


What You Need

-Foam Board (at least 24 inch x 24 inch )

-Poster frame (at least 20 inch x 16 inch )

-Clear Plastic Sheets

-Card Stock
-SpeedDeck Logo

-Card Place Holder Template (Place Holder Template.pdf)
-Exacto Knife

-Glue
-Scissors



Constructing the Game Board

1. Print out 5 copies of the Card Place Holder Template and 1 copy of the logo onto card stock.

2. Using your scissors, cut out each all 26 place holders and the logo.

3. Arrange the place holders and logo on the Foam Board, according to the picture.

4. Once you have all the place holders and logo where they should be, glue them to the foam board.
5. Using the Exacto knife, cut off all the extra foam board, leaving roughly a 24 inch x 24 inch square board.

6. Using your scissors, cut the clear plastic sheet into a square the exact same size as your board (24 inch x 24 inch )

7. Place the freshly cut plastic sheet over the game board and slide each side of the poster frame onto the the game board and plastic.

8. Play!
